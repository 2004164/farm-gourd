What We Do?

FarmGuard ensures real-time detection, analysis, and mitigation of animal intrusions. Advanced technologies and user-friendly interfaces provide robust protection for crops, enabling prompt responses and improved agricultural security.

  1. Detection: IR sensors detect movement
  2. Sound Activation: Sensors detect sounds
  3. Camera Activation: Cameras record the movement
  4. Analysis: Software identifies a wild boar; AI predicts intrusion
  5. Response: Acoustic deterrents activate to scare the boar away
  6. Notification: Farmer receives an alert and reviews footage via the app
